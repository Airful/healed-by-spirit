Somewhere underneath the noise, there's a version of you that knows exactly what it's doing. The Connect & Radiate course is seven modules of work designed to help you get back in touch with that, and stay there.

It's not easy. Brian calls it "beautiful and challenging," and from what past students describe, both words are accurate. This isn't about learning new ideas. It's about reconnecting with something you already carry.

## The seven modules

Each module builds on the one before it. Here's what the arc looks like:

### Module 1: Connection beyond the norm

You start with the Soul Connection at Will Workshop. This is where you learn to step outside the usual way we interact, the polite surface-level stuff, and experience something more direct.

### Module 2: Our essential nature — awareness

The focus moves to awareness itself. Not your thoughts, not your emotions, but the awareness behind them. Most people are surprised by how different this feels from what they expected.

### Module 3: Expanding and maturing the awareness

Once you've had a taste of it, the work is to make it stick. This module is about stabilizing what you touched in Module 2 so it becomes a home base, not a one-off experience.

### Module 4: Life inside

Here you turn inward. Your brain's thoughts, your body's sensations, the full experience of being alive from the inside. Intuition and creativity live here, and this module is about learning to access them on purpose.

### Module 5: As within, so without

What's happening inside you starts to change what's happening around you. This module is about that relationship, how your inner state shapes your outer life, and what shifts when you bring awareness to it.

### Module 6: Consciously co-creating with Creator

This is where things go deeper. You move from observing to participating, learning to partner with the creative intelligence of life itself. Brian describes it as "manifesting the life of your dreams," and by this point in the course, that language starts to feel less abstract.

### Module 7: Putting it all together

The last module is about integration. How do you take everything from the previous six modules and actually live from it? You leave with a practice, not just an experience.
