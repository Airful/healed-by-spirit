Most of us have heard it at some point. A quiet knowing that cuts through the noise, just for a second, before the mental chatter drowns it out again. You're left wondering: was that real? Can I get back there?

That's what the Soul Connection at Will Workshop is about. Getting back there, and learning to stay.

## Mind chatter vs. soul talk

The workshop starts with an exercise that, honestly, catches most people off guard. You learn to tell the difference between "mind chatter" and "soul talk." Mind chatter is the running commentary: the worries, the judgments, the constant second-guessing. Soul talk is quieter. It's clear in a way that the mind isn't, and it carries a kind of truth you can feel in your body.

Once you can tell them apart, things start to shift. Not in some dramatic overnight way, but in how you make decisions, how you listen, how you show up with the people in your life.

## The sacred space

This isn't a lecture. It's something you have to experience.

All participants agree to honor a level of safety, vulnerability, and confidentiality. That agreement matters. When people feel safe enough to drop the performance, real connection happens. People who walked in as strangers start to actually see each other.

> "When you release what's in the way, what remains is who you are." — Brian Kurtz

## What you'll leave with

By the end of the workshop, you'll have:

- A felt understanding of the difference between mind chatter and soul talk
- Tools you can use on your own to access that quieter place
- A direct experience of genuine connection with the people in the room
- A clearer sense of who you are when the noise falls away

These aren't concepts to think about later. They're things you'll feel during the workshop itself.

## Who comes to these?

Families, couples, individuals, corporate teams. Some people come because a relationship needs help. Others come because they feel stuck and can't quite name why. Business groups come because genuine trust between colleagues changes how a team works, and you can't get there through icebreakers.
